Entrepreneur: working on a tech startup idea

eRoboVocale

I am planning on launching my first tech startup, eRoboVocale. My business model is based on a social media app that can make users’ voices sound amazing. It is similar to TikTok in that you can share your music with others. This app comes with built-in powerful vocal processing tools (like Autotune) that automatically detect music keys and adjust everything (EQ, reverb, etc.) based on your needs. You can rap and sing and don’t even need a microphone. The app will be available on App Store and Google Playstore.
